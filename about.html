Track & Field Photo & Video

I’m Barry Clavin, the lens behind bc_trackvision. What started on the infield with DCU Athletics evolved into a full-time focus on capturing Ireland’s fastest moments — from stadium lights to coastal roads.

As Chairperson of DCU Athletics Club, I know the sport’s heartbeat. That insight shapes every frame: the anticipation on the start line, the rhythm of baton exchanges, the roar of the finish.
